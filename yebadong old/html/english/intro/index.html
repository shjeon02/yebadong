ntroducing Yebadong

  • Yebadong is a Korean mailing list which is composed of a group of people who are very interested in progressive rock music. Yebadong was born in April 7th, 1994.
  • The word Yebadong came from a Korean word Ye-Sul-Ba-We-Dong-Ho-Hoi which means artrock mania club.
  • The address of our mailing list is yebadong@onelist.com. If you have news or questions about progressive rock music, please mail to this address without hesitation. But mailing is requiring registration to onelist. Posting is also available. You can visit and post at web board, Square. Our knowledge is so limited !!

ubscribing to YeBaDong
