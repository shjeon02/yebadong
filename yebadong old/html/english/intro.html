What is YeBaDong ??

  • YBD is a korean mailinglist which is composed of a group of people who are very very interested in progressive music. YBD was born in April 7th, 1994.

  • The word YeBaDong came from a korean word Ye-Sul-Ba-We-Dong-Ho-Hoi which means "a group of people who are interested in artrock music."

  • The address of our mailinglist is yebadong@cclab.kaist.ac.kr. If you have news or questions about progressive/artrock music, please mail to this address without hesitation. Our knowledge is so limited!!

  • YBD is connetted to han.arts.music.progressive(one of korean newsgroups) bi-directionally. Thank you for your interest.

    Subscribing to YBD

  • If you are to get in YBD, please mail to majordomo@cclab.kaist.ac.kr. Two choices are availabe.
      subscribe yebadong
      subscribe yebadong-digest
    with the following informations of your
      Real Name
      Job
      Phone No.
      E-Mail Address

  • You are in YBD mailinglist as soon as your informations are accepted. Please subscribe to YBD now!! Thank you.
